SAP HANA On Demand for Premise and Cloud

SAP HANA is an in-memory data platform that is deployable as an on-premise appliance, or in the cloud.
It is a revolutionary platform, which is best suited for performing real-time analytics, developing and deploying real-time applications.
At the core of this real-time data platform is the SAP HANA database, which is fundamentally different from any other database engine in the market today.
